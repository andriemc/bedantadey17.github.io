FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

# How do I download and install the packs?

Once you have selected your desired packs and downloaded them, unzip the zip using a software like 7-Zip or WinRAR. You will observe the packs you have selected in the site.

Windows:

After that, select those packs and copy them. Then press Win+R to open the run command box, and type in %appdata%/.minecraft/resourcepacks and paste those packs there.

Linux:

.minecraft is located in ~/.minecraft. As it's a hidden folder, you may need to enable the "Display Hidden Files" or press Ctrl+H in your file manager. Then navigate to resourcepacks and paste the files there.

# What is Cosmopacks?

Cosmopacks is inspired by VanillaTweaks. These resourcepacks aim to fix/add features to vanilla Minecraft without making drastic changes. You'll find these changes very subtle and productive in many scenarios.

I hope you will find these packs interesting and useful, as a lot of time goes into developing them.
